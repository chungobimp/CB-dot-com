Design and Project Management for Websites and Software

The stress is unbearable. You wonder how and why you got into this mess. Over budget, behind schedule and all the needed features are not yet built ... and the business really needs this to work ...

It is very easy to dive straight into an IT project, small or large, and get lost along the way.
It is very easy to get carried away with an on-going project and lose track of the important things that need doing.
Though keeping on top of these sounds obvious and simple, it can be a lot harder than many might think, resulting on costly over-runs, defeated budgets and project shortfalls.

ChungoBimp provides a host of services for your IT project to ensure it all goes well. From Strategy, Inception and Planning through full scale Design and Project Management all the way to Implementation, Deployment and beyond. Whether you choose one or all of our services, ChungoBimp will help you make the most of the project.

Getting it right first time

When budgets, time and a business depend on the success of a project, there is only one opportunity to get it right. The chances of project success are greatly improved by splitting projects into four key stages. Each one will play an integral part of an overall project so missing out or paying scant regard to one will impact on the outcome of your project...

Strategy

Before you start any project, no matter how big or small, you need a strategy; Why are you embarking on this project, what do you want to achieve and what is it that you need to do in order to accomplish it?

Devise Your Strategy ...

Design

Many people enjoy this stage, but few know how to do it properly! Designing your IT project well is essential to ensure you get what you need and will provides you with the necessary understanding your entire scheme.

Create Your Design ...

Plan

Planning your IT project is key to making sure it all comes out the other end smelling of roses. Basic planning can be done with a Strategy and high level design, full detailed planning can only occur with detailed design.

Plan Your Project ...

Manage

So you have a Strategy, you have a detailed Design and you have a Plan, now it's time to manage all of the elements. Without management your project can easily be delayed or run off course ending in a right 'ol mess.
