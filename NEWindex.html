Hello!  It's good to see you :)

The stress is unbearable. You wonder how and why you got into this mess. Over budget, behind schedule and all the needed features are not built yet and you company needs this to work...

It is very easy to dive straight into an IT project, small or large, without thinking about what is actually needed, how it will be done and when you want it.
Though this sounds obvious and simple, it can be a lot harder than you might think. Spending the right amount of time, effort and resource on the 4 key stages in a project is essential to get what you want, when you want it and within the right budget.
ChungoBimp provides you with the key tools to ensure these stages are well thought out, administered and actioned.

ChungoBimp provides a host of services for your IT project. From Strategy, Inception and Planning through full scale Design, Project Management and Training all the way to Implementation, Deployment and beyond.
Whether you choose all or just one of our services, ChungoBimp will be the wisest choice you make for your project.
